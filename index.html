How It All Started

After High school I began to take an interest in computer programming. When I moved to Atlanta one of my mentors who was intrigued on how much I was interested in programming recommended me to a coding program called Code Start. Through Code Start I went through an intensive 3 month coding bootcamp at the Iron Yard and became a Junior Java Developer. The bootcamp began my professional experience and taught me a lot of valuable lessons, but most importantly it got me interested in pursuing a career in Development.

Shortly after graduating from the Iron Yard, I was hired by a startup called PeachDish, where I spent my first couple of months building landing pages and websites although I did not have much Front-End experience. In addition to that, during my time at Peachdish, I was also part of the Digital Marketing & Analytics team where I created ads using tools like Google Adwords, Google Tag manager and more. I also ran my own Ad campaigns. From there my interest in Front-End development grew and for the next couple of months I began teaching myself more HTML, CSS, and Javascript by taking all of the front-end Codecademy and Code Camp courses online.
